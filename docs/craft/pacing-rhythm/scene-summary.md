## ğŸ“Š **Scene-Summary Ratio by Genre**

- **Scene:** 80-90%

### **Literary Fiction:**
- **Scene:** 60-70%

- **Why:** Reflection and internal process need compression

- **Scene:** 70-75%
- **Summary:** 25-30%
- **Why:** World-building and travel require summary

- **Scene:** 75-85%

- **Why:** Emotional moments need full scenes

### **Historical Fiction:**
- **Scene:** 65-75%
- **Summary:** 25-35%
- **Why:** Background context requires more summary

**Note:** These are guidelines, not rules. Story needs dictate ratio.

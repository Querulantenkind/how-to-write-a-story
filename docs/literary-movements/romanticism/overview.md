## âœ¨ **Core Principles**

### **1. Emotion Over Reason**
Feelings are a path to truth, not obstacles to it.

**In Practice:**
- Characters driven by passion, not logic
- Emotional intensity valued over restraint
- Intuition as wisdom
- Emphasis on subjective experience

### **2. Individual vs. Society**
The unique individual against conformist society.

**In Practice:**
- Outsider protagonists
- Misunderstood geniuses
- Rebellion against social norms
- Celebration of uniqueness

### **3. Nature as Teacher**
Nature reveals profound truths unavailable in civilization.

**In Practice:**
- Sublime landscapes
- Nature as spiritual force
- Contrast between corrupt city and pure countryside
- Symbolic use of natural imagery

### **4. Imagination Supreme**
Imagination can access deeper truths than observation.

**In Practice:**
- Fantasy and supernatural elements
- Dreams and visions as valid experiences
- Creative expression as sacred
- The artist as visionary

### **5. Past and Exotic**
Looking backward or outward for inspiration.

**In Practice:**
- Medieval settings
- Folk tales and legends
- Oriental exoticism
- Mythological references

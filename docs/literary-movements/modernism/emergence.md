## âœï¸ **Modernist Techniques**

### **In Medias Res**
Start in the middle of action, no exposition.

### **Epiphany**
Joyce's term: moment of sudden revelation or insight.

### **Objective Correlative**
Eliot's concept: external situation expressing emotion.

### **Juxtaposition**
Place contrasting elements side by side without transition.

### **Allusion**
Dense references to other literature, mythology, history.

### **Interior Monologue**
Direct presentation of unspoken thoughts.

### **Free Association**
Thoughts connected by logic of association, not narrative logic.

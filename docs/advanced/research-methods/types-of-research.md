## ğŸ’¡ **Pro Tips**

### **1. Start Broad, Then Narrow**
Begin with general overview sources, then drill into specifics.

### **2. Cross-Reference Everything**
One source might be wrong. Three sources confirming? Probably accurate.

### **3. Follow the Footnotes**
Bibliographies and citations lead to deeper sources.

### **4. Ask "What Would My Character Know?"**
Research what your POV character would realistically understand.

### **5. Notice What Surprises You**
Your surprises often make the best details for readers.

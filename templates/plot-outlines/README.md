## ğŸ†˜ **Troubleshooting**

### **"My story doesn't fit any template!"**
That's okay! Templates are **descriptive, not prescriptive**. Use them as tools, not rules. Your unique story might need a unique structure.

### **"I feel constrained by the template"**
Remember: templates serve your story, not the other way around. Adapt freely or try a different one.

Begin with the simplest template ([Basic Story Outline](basic-story-outline.md)) and build from there.

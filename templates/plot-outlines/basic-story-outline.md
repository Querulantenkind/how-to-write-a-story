> *A flexible template for planning any story from premise to resolution*

This universal outline template helps you structure your story from initial concept to final climax. Use it for any genre, any length‚Äîfrom short stories to novels. Fill in as much or as little detail as you need at your current planning stage.

---

## üéØ **Using This Template**

<details>
<summary><strong>How Story Outlines Work</strong></summary>

### **The Purpose:**
- **Clarify your story's shape** before writing
- **Identify plot holes** early
- **Maintain story logic** and causality
- **Ensure pacing** and escalation
- **Track multiple storylines** (plot, subplots, character arcs)

### **Flexibility Levels:**
- **Plotter:** Fill out every section in detail
- **Pantser:** Use only high-level sections, discover details while writing
- **Plantser:** Fill major beats, leave room for discovery

### **Not Set in Stone:**
Your outline will evolve as you write. That's normal and healthy.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Step-by-Step Process</strong></summary>

### **Planning Order:**
1. **Start with premise** ‚Äî Core concept
2. **Define protagonist** ‚Äî Who changes?
3. **Establish conflict** ‚Äî What opposes them?
4. **Map beginning, middle, end** ‚Äî Three-part structure
5. **Add turning points** ‚Äî Major plot beats
6. **Include character arc** ‚Äî Internal journey
7. **Develop subplots** ‚Äî Secondary storylines
8. **Scene-by-scene** (optional) ‚Äî Detailed breakdown

### **Tips:**
- ‚úÖ Focus on causality (because X, then Y)
- ‚úÖ Include emotional beats, not just plot
- ‚úÖ Note pacing (fast/slow sections)
- ‚úÖ Leave room for discovery
- ‚ùå Don't over-plan if it kills creativity
- ‚ùå Don't skip the ending

</details>

Before writing, verify:

- [ ] **Clear protagonist with goal and flaw**
- [ ] **Strong inciting incident that disrupts status quo**
- [ ] **Escalating conflicts and obstacles**
- [ ] **Midpoint that raises stakes**
- [ ] **Dark night/all is lost moment**
- [ ] **Protagonist's internal and external arcs intersect**

- [ ] **Resolution provides satisfaction**
- [ ] **Cause and effect chain is logical**
- [ ] **Subplots enhance main story**
- [ ] **Theme is woven throughout**
- [ ] **Pacing varies appropriately**

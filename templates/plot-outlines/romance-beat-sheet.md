# üíï Romance Beat Sheet

> *Structure your romance story with genre-specific beats*

Romance has its own story logic with specific emotional beats readers expect. This template helps you craft a satisfying romance arc that delivers on the genre's promise while creating unique, memorable love stories.

---

## üéØ **Understanding Romance Structure**

<details>
<summary><strong>Essential Romance Elements</strong></summary>

### **The Romance Promise:**
- **Two people meet and fall in love**
- **External and internal conflicts keep them apart**
- **They overcome obstacles together**
- **The relationship ends stronger (HEA or HFN)**

### **Critical Requirements:**
- ‚úÖ **Emotional focus** ‚Äî Relationship is the main plot
- ‚úÖ **Optimistic ending** ‚Äî HEA (Happily Ever After) or HFN (Happy For Now)
- ‚úÖ **Mutual arc** ‚Äî Both characters grow
- ‚úÖ **Central conflict** ‚Äî Why can't they be together?
- ‚úÖ **Chemistry** ‚Äî Attraction must be tangible

### **Romance-Specific Beats:**
Unlike general story structure, romance focuses on:
- Meeting/attraction
- Building connection
- Emotional barriers
- The dark moment (relationship breaking point)
- Grand gesture/declaration
- Commitment

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>How to Use This Template</strong></summary>

### **Planning Process:**
1. **Define both protagonists** (dual POV or single)
2. **Establish their wounds/barriers**
3. **Create conflict that keeps them apart**
4. **Map emotional progression**
5. **Plan the dark moment carefully**
6. **Ensure satisfying resolution**

### **Tips:**
- ‚úÖ Make both characters equally important
- ‚úÖ Show attraction early
- ‚úÖ Create believable conflict
- ‚úÖ Give each character an arc
- ‚úÖ Build sexual/romantic tension
- ‚ùå Don't rely on misunderstandings alone
- ‚ùå Don't make conflict easily solved by talking

</details>

## ‚úÖ **Romance Requirements Checklist**

Ensure your romance delivers:

### **Structure:**
- [ ] Meet-cute is memorable
- [ ] Attraction shown early
- [ ] Forced proximity/stuck together
- [ ] Relationship deepens at midpoint
- [ ] Dark moment is earned and painful
- [ ] Grand gesture is meaningful
- [ ] HEA/HFN is satisfying

### **Characters:**
- [ ] Both protagonists have full arcs
- [ ] Both change and grow
- [ ] Wounds/fears are established
- [ ] Internal conflicts are real
- [ ] Chemistry is palpable
- [ ] They make each other better

### **Conflict:**
- [ ] Central conflict is believable
- [ ] Can't be solved by one conversation
- [ ] Escalates naturally
- [ ] Both internal and external obstacles
- [ ] Dark moment tests commitment
- [ ] Resolution addresses core issues

### **Emotion:**
- [ ] Emotional beats are clear
- [ ] Reader feels the tension
- [ ] Vulnerability is shown
- [ ] Joy and pain are balanced
- [ ] Ending is emotionally satisfying
- [ ] Genre promise fulfilled (optimistic ending)

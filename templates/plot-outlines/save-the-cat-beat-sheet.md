## üìñ **About This Template**

The **Save the Cat Beat Sheet**, created by screenwriter Blake Snyder, is one of the most popular story structures in both Hollywood and publishing. It provides **15 specific beats** that create a satisfying, engaging narrative arc.

### **Best For:**
- Commercial fiction (thrillers, romance, fantasy, sci-fi)
- Plot-driven stories
- Writers who want detailed structure
- Stories needing strong pacing
- Manuscripts around 90,000-100,000 words

## üí° **TIPS FOR USING SAVE THE CAT**

<details>
<summary><b>Expert Advice</b></summary>

### **DO:**
- ‚úÖ Use percentages as flexible guidelines
- ‚úÖ Adjust for your specific story
- ‚úÖ Return to beat sheet if story feels off
- ‚úÖ Use for diagnosis in revision
- ‚úÖ Pay special attention to key beats (Catalyst, Midpoint, All Is Lost, Break Into Three)

### **DON'T:**
- ‚ùå Force beats that don't fit
- ‚ùå Obsess over exact word counts
- ‚ùå Make beats obvious/mechanical
- ‚ùå Ignore your story's needs
- ‚ùå Treat as rigid formula

### **Remember:**
> "The beat sheet is a tool, not a tyrant. Use it to enhance your story, not constrain it."

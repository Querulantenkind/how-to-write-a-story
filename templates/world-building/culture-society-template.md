## üéØ Overview

Culture is the soul of your world. This template helps you create societies that feel authentic, complex, and internally consistent. Use it for each major culture in your story, focusing most energy on cultures your characters belong to or interact with significantly.

**Time Investment:** 1-2 hours per culture (minimum); more for major cultures  
**Best For:** Any story where cultural dynamics matter  
**Remember:** Cultures are not monolithic‚Äîbuild in diversity and contradictions

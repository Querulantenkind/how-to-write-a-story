## ğŸ’¡ Revision Tips & Troubleshooting

### When You're Stuck

**Problem:** Can't see the forest for the trees
- Take a complete break (minimum 1 week)
- Write a one-page summary of your story from memory
- Compare what you remember to what's on the page

**Problem:** Every sentence feels wrong
- You may be over-editing â€” step back to bigger picture
- Read published books in your genre to recalibrate
- Focus on one specific issue per pass

**Problem:** Not sure what to cut
- If you're not sure, mark it and move on
- Ask: "If I cut this, what would the reader miss?"
- Trust your beta readers to confirm or deny

**Problem:** Revisions making it worse
- Compare to earlier draft â€” you may be over-editing
- Identify what's actually wrong vs. what you're tired of
- Get outside perspective

### Signs You're Done

âœ… Changes are getting smaller and smaller  
âœ… You're making things different, not better  
âœ… Beta readers have no major concerns  
âœ… You feel confident sharing it  
âœ… You're excited about what's next (not just exhausted)

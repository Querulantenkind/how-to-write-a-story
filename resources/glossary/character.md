## **Protagonist**

The character whose goal drives the main story problem.

## **Antagonist**

The person/force opposing the protagonistâ€™s goal (not necessarily a villain).

## **External Goal (Want)**

A concrete objective the character pursues in the plot.

## **Internal Need**

A psychological/emotional lesson or change the character must confront.

## **Motivation**

The reason the character pursues a goal; what makes it matter.

## **Flaw**

A trait, belief, or habit that creates trouble and pressure for change.

## **Character Arc**

The pattern of internal change across the story.

## **Positive Arc**

The character grows into a healthier belief or identity.

## **Negative Arc**

The character embraces a harmful belief or becomes morally worse.

## **Flat Arc**

The character remains largely stable internally, but changes the world/others around them.

## **Backstory**

Relevant past events that shape present behavior.

## **The Ghost**

A defining past wound/event that continues to influence the character.

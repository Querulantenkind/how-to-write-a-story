> *Terms for querying, editing, rights, and the publication pipeline*

---

## **Query Letter**

A pitch letter sent to an agent/editor summarizing the book and credentials.

## **Synopsis**

A concise summary of the full plot, including the ending.

## **Manuscript (MS)**

The full text of your book, prepared for submission.

## **Beta Reader**

A test reader who gives feedback from a reader perspective.

## **Developmental Edit**

Big-picture editing focused on structure, pacing, character, and story logic.

## **Line Edit**

Sentence-level editing for clarity, flow, voice, and style.

## **Copy Edit**

Technical editing for grammar, consistency, and correctness.

## **Proofreading**

Final error check after layout/formatting.

## **Rights**

Legal permissions to publish/translate/adapt a work.

## **Advance**

Money paid upfront by a publisher (typically recouped from royalties).

## **Royalties**

Ongoing payments based on sales or licensing.

## **Comp Titles (Comps)**

Comparable books used to position your project in the market.

### **Simple Drafting**

- risks: long manuscripts can get messy without structure

### **Long-Form Project Managers**

Good when you want chapters/scenes, notes, research in one place.

- strengths: structure, navigation, planning integration
- risks: setup overhead; tool can become procrastination

Good when you want plain-text longevity.

- strengths: portable, version-control friendly
- risks: exporting/formatting can take extra steps
